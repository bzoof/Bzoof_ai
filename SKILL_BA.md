This skill helps a business analyst collect stakeholder needs, define requirements, validate assumptions, and prepare a clear scope for implementation.

## When to use
- capturing requirements for a new product, feature, or initiative
- aligning business objectives, user needs, and technical constraints
- preparing validated documentation for development and stakeholder review

## Workflow
1. Identify business objectives and stakeholders
   - gather key goals, success metrics, and stakeholder roles
   - understand the value proposition and desired outcomes
   - document assumptions, constraints, and regulatory needs
2. Clarify the problem and target users
   - define the user personas, user journeys, and pain points
   - articulate the business problem and why it matters
   - capture existing processes or systems that the solution should improve
3. Define requirements and acceptance criteria
   - translate user needs into functional and non-functional requirements
   - prioritize requirements by business impact and risk
   - write clear acceptance criteria and success measures for each requirement
4. Validate and iterate
   - review requirements with stakeholders and subject-matter experts
   - identify gaps, conflicts, and dependencies
   - refine the scope and update the documentation
5. Prepare delivery-ready artifacts
   - produce a requirements summary, user stories, and a decision log
   - include risks, constraints, assumptions, and next steps
   - align the documentation with the project or sprint plan

## Completion criteria
- documented business objectives and stakeholder alignment
- clear problem statement, user needs, and personas
- prioritized requirements with acceptance criteria
- identified risks, constraints, and dependencies
- a validated scope and follow-up actions for delivery

## Questions to ask
- What are the key business goals and success metrics?
- Who are the primary users and what are their top pain points?
- What assumptions or constraints must we respect?
- What should be included in the minimum viable delivery?
- Which stakeholders need to review or approve the requirements?
