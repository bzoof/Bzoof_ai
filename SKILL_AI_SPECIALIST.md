This skill helps an AI specialist support product, engineering, and design teams as they build an AI application, from problem framing to model selection and deployment readiness.

## When to use
- defining the AI use case and product value for a new application
- selecting models, data strategy, and evaluation criteria
- coordinating AI-related requirements across product, engineering, and operations

## Workflow
1. Define the AI problem and product value
   - clarify the goal, expected user outcomes, and business impact
   - identify the AI use case, value proposition, and success metrics
   - capture assumptions, data availability, and domain constraints
2. Assess data and model requirements
   - evaluate available data sources, quality, and labeling needs
   - select model architectures or services that match the use case
   - identify training, inference, fine-tuning, and evaluation requirements
3. Design the AI solution and integration
   - define inputs, outputs, user interactions, and system flows
   - specify how AI will integrate with backend, frontend, and UX
   - include safety, explainability, and privacy controls
4. Validate and iterate with stakeholders
   - review the AI strategy with product, engineering, and business teams
   - surface risks, biases, and operational constraints
   - refine the approach based on feedback, feasibility, and tuning needs
5. Prepare implementation and operational readiness
   - document model selection, fine-tuning approach, data pipeline, evaluation plan, and deployment needs
   - define monitoring, retraining, and performance metrics
   - ensure alignment with sprint planning and delivery readiness

## Completion criteria
- a clear AI use case and business value statement
- documented data, model, and fine-tuning strategy
- integration plan for AI within the application architecture
- risk, bias, privacy, and operational controls defined
- implementation-ready artifacts and success metrics

## Questions to ask
- What user problem should the AI feature solve?
- What data is available and how will it be prepared?
- What model behavior, accuracy, and performance requirements matter most?
- How should the AI feature be validated and monitored in production?
- What product, security, or ethical constraints should be respected?
